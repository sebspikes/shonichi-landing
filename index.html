01 — The problem

Your guests are telling you everything. You're not hearing it.

Professional STR operators handle hundreds of guest messages every month. Most are routine. But buried in that volume are signals that cost you money, ratings, and repeat bookings. We know this because we found it in our own data.

Clare Street, Bristol. Six units, one building.

The front three face the street. The back three don't. Dozens of reviews from the front units mentioned noise. Dozens from the back praised how quiet it was. We'd been reading these reviews individually for years. Never once saw the pattern.

Hampton Park 56a. Our best unit. 4.9 rating. Guest favourite badge.

When we pulled the data, it was declining. Recent quarters showed more lower reviews than any previous period. Cleaning issues flagged by the same guest across two separate stays. We would have told you 56a was bulletproof. It wasn't.

Response times. We thought we were fast.

Our median response time was 4 minutes. Our average was 132 minutes. The median looked fine. The average told the real story: overnight gaps, weekend dead zones, messages sitting for hours when life got in the way. Those are the ones that hurt reviews.
